## 👾 UKB

> Electrical engineer. AI builder. Polymath by accident.

I've been taking things apart since before school — tools in a garage in Tashkent,
terminals everywhere after.

Most people are surprised when they find out I know chess, F1, literature, basketball,
and authors they've never heard of. I stopped being surprised a long time ago.
Curiosity doesn't pick a lane.

I don't combine random things. I combine things that work separately but are **invisible
together** — that gap is where the real product lives. FlatMate started there.
So will everything after it.

I cut social media out. I let boredom do the thinking.
I read Deep Work and still fight focus every single day — at least I know what I'm fighting.

**"Tomorrow" is a word I removed from my vocabulary. It means never.**

## 🚀 What I'm Building

### 🏠 FlatMate

> Started as a dashboard for my apartment. Becoming something bigger.

The idea isn't just an apartment management app —
it's the foundation of a **marketplace connecting renters and landlords**,
the way LinkedIn connects people and opportunities.
Right now: real-time expenses, tasks, cleaning schedules, announcements,
currency rates, and a Telegram bot. Built for real roommates. Tested by us.
